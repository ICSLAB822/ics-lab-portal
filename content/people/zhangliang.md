In September 2024, graduated from the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, obtaining a doctoral degree in Computer Science and Technology. My supervisor was Professor Fei Anyi and Professor Zhu Hongzi. From September 2021 to December 2022, I was jointly trained in the Laboratory of Computational Science of Professor Cao Jiannong at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. In November 2024, joined the School of Computer Science and Technology of Donghua University as an instructor. My main research directions include parallel and distributed computing, stream computing, cloud-edge resource scheduling, and neural network system optimization. My research achievements have been published in international authoritative journals and conferences in related fields, including CCF A-class journals and conferences TPDS, TC, INFOCOM, TMC, etc., as well as B-class conferences IPDPS, ICPP, ICDCS, etc. I serve as a reviewer for journals such as TPDS, TC, and IoTJ.

2024年9月毕业于上海交通大学电子信息与电气工程学院，获计算机科学与技术专业博士学位，师从过敏意教授和朱弘恣教授。2021年9月-2022年12月在香港理工大学计算学系曹建农教授实验室联合培养。2024年11月加入东华大学计算机科学与技术学院担任讲师。主要研究方向为并行与分布式计算、流式计算、云边资源调度和神经网络系统优化。研究成果发表在相关领域国际权威期刊和会议，其中包括CCF A类期刊和会议 TPDS、TC、INFOCOM、TMC等，B类会议IPDPS、ICPP、ICDCS等。担任TPDS、TC、IoTJ等期刊审稿人。
