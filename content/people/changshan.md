Prof. Shan Chang is a Professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Donghua University. Her research interests primarily include privacy protection, mobile crowdsensing, and Internet of Things security. She has published numerous papers in top-tier journals and conferences such as IEEE TMC, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TPDS, and INFOCOM. She serves as a reviewer for multiple authoritative international journals.

常姗，东华大学计算机科学与技术学院教授，博士生导师。主要研究方向包括隐私保护、移动群智感知、物联网安全等。在 IEEE TMC, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TPDS, INFOCOM 等顶级期刊和会议上发表多篇高水平论文。担任多个国际权威期刊审稿人。
